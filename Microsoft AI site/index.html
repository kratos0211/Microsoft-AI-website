Frequently Asked Questions

What is Microsoft Copilot for M365?

Microsoft Copilot is an AI-powered productivity tool that uses large language models (LLMs) and integrates your data with the Microsoft 365 apps you use every day.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Copilot automatically inherits your organization's security, compliance, and privacy policies. Your data is not used to train the public models.

What are the license requirements?

Copilot is available as an add-on for Microsoft 365 Business Standard, Business Premium, E3, and E5 customers. There is no longer a minimum seat requirement.

How do I get technical support?
